Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

General Online Safety
- Be careful about what you share online. Avoid posting personal details like phone number, address, or bank information.
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links, especially those sent through WhatsApp, SMS, or email.
- Use strong passwords and avoid using the same password for multiple accounts.
- Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible for extra security.
- Avoid logging into important accounts while using public Wi-Fi.

UPI & Financial Safety
- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.
- Always remember: you only enter your UPI PIN to SEND money, not to receive it.
- Do not accept payment requests from unknown people.
- Before sending money, always check the name and details of the receiver carefully.
- Avoid scanning QR codes from random or untrusted sources.

Device & App Safety
- Download apps only from trusted sources like the Play Store or App Store.
- Keep your phone and apps updated to avoid security risks.
- Be careful while giving permissions to apps; allow only what is necessary.

Awareness & Reporting
- Stay aware of common scams like fake calls, messages, and payment requests.
- If something feels suspicious, trust your instincts and avoid taking action immediately.

If you become a victim of cybercrime, report it immediately:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
