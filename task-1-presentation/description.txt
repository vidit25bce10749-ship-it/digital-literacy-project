Task: Digital Literacy Infographic

Overview:
- Designed a Digital Literacy Awareness infographic using Canva.
- The aim was to present important concepts in a simple and visually engaging format.

What I Did:
- Included topics like digital literacy, safe internet practices, useful tools, and email etiquette.
- Used icons, colors, and layout to make the content easy to understand.
- Focused on clarity and readability while designing.

Learning & Reflection:
- Learned how to present information visually in an effective way.
- Improved my creativity and design thinking skills.
- Faced a challenge in organizing multiple ideas without making the design crowded.
- This task helped me understand the importance of visual communication in learning.
