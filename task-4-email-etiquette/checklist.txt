Task: Email Etiquette

Overview:
- Focused on learning professional email communication and proper etiquette.

Do's:
- Use a clear and relevant subject line.
- Start with a polite and respectful greeting.
- Keep the message clear, concise, and to the point.
- Use a professional tone throughout the email.
- End with a proper closing and signature.

Don'ts:
- Avoid using informal language or slang.
- Do not send emails without checking spelling and grammar.
- Avoid writing unclear or incomplete messages.
- Do not make emails unnecessarily long.
- Do not forget to include important details.

Learning & Reflection:
- Learned how to write structured and professional emails.
- Understood the importance of clarity and politeness in communication.
- Realized that proper email etiquette creates a positive impression.
- This skill will be useful in both academic and professional life.
